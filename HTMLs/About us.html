About Us

Over 33 years of service!

Founded in 1989 as American Export Lines, rebranded to SHIPIT Logistics® in 2021 to encompass our growing capabilities, we continue to offer full-service international freight forwarding and logistics services worldwide. We provide transportation services to shippers, forwarders, indirect air carriers, and property brokers.


Licenses:


  • FMC Licensed as an Ocean Transportation Intermediary NVOCC with Org# 004869 and License# 001803
  • FMCSA Licensed Broker of Property with USDOT# 2629896 and MC License# MC-910043 and FMCSA Licensed Freight Forwarder with USDOT# 2629896 and FF# 53067
  • IATA/CNS Licensed as an Indirect Air Carrier/Air Forwarder with IATA/CNS# 01134480012
  • TSA Licensed Indirect Air Carrier

Customer service is our first priority, led by our competitive rates and operational excellency. Our values are Accountability, Proactiveness, Professionalism and Respect. Being a professional freight forwarder is not enough at SHIPIT Logistics®, we believe in our clients as partners; their success is our success. Constant attention to expediting your shipment is just part of the routine for us.


ESG, and in particular environmental requirements for shippers is more relevant than ever, please ask your rep about how SHIPIT can offer visibility of your carbon emissions and provide carbon offsets against your freight supply chain with us.


We are proud of and thankful for the loyalty of our employees, most of whom have been our teammates for over 10 years.

Our Mission:


To deliver an exceptional customer experience through respect, integrity, reliability, professionalism, attention to detail, and competitive pricing using effective communication, having a passion for our work, and a dedication to our partners and customers worldwide.


The Vision:


To be the most customer-centric, operationally excellent and trusted logistics company to our partners and customers worldwide.


Our network of Partners provides a global reach for all your shipments, worldwide.

SHIPIT Logistics® is unique in its approach to worldwide operations and coverage. As an American company that has been in business since 1974, we have built a network of competent, experienced, responsible, trustworthy, agile, and competitive partners who are the extension of SHIPIT. We have built these partnerships over years of business together with these partners, meeting them face to face, understanding each other's strengths and professional certifications, and developing and implementing operations that sync with our customer's demands.


Our global partners each specialize in certain areas of the supply chain, logistics and freight forwarding. They each have their strengths to secure space with ocean and air carriers, develop in-country warehousing and distribution, secure the right truckers, develop IT and Track/Trace systems, and clear customs.


Our global reach gives our customers peace of mind that their shipments, whether heavy lift/project cargo, ocean freight, air freight, trucking or warehousing, or a combination of these modes, will be taken care of from A-Z.
