OUR SERVICES

STORAGE

At eCargo Logistics, we understand that safe and efficient storage is a critical component of the logistics and supply chain process. Our comprehensive storage solutions are designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients, providing secure, well-managed storage facilities to safeguard your goods and materials. With a commitment to excellence, we offer a range of storage services to optimize your supply chain operations.

Our Storage Facilities

Warehousing Excellence: Our state-of-the-art warehouses are strategically located to provide proximity to major transportation hubs, reducing transit times and costs. These facilities come in various sizes and configurations to accommodate the unique requirements of your inventory.
Yard Storage: In addition to indoor storage, we offer secure outdoor storage yards for items like vehicles, machinery, and bulk materials. Our yards are equipped with the latest security measures to ensure the safety of your assets.
Container Storage: If you require container storage, we have the capacity to store cargo containers of various sizes in a secure environment. Your containers will be readily available when you need them for transportation.
